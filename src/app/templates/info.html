What is Schedulo?

Schedulo provides an intuitive tool to effortlessly plan your study schedule and organise your timetable
Schedulo is designed to help you avoid clashes, boost productivity and reduce stress so you can focus
on what matters most: your studies

How It Works

  • Select Your Units – Enter subjects & key details.
  • Customize Your Schedule – Adjust times, priorities, and deadlines.
  • Visualize Your Timetable – Instantly see your weekly plan.
  • Optimize Productivity – Get suggestions & avoid conflicts.

Key Features

  • Smart Scheduling – Detect clashes automatically and suggest optimized timetables.
  • Visual Timetable – Interactive calendar with color-coded events.
  • Customizable Units – Add subjects, priorities, and deadlines.
  • Collaboration Tools – Share schedules with classmates or colleagues.
  • Notifications & Reminders – Stay on track with smart alerts.
