Welcome to Schedulo!

Schedulo is your ultimate tool for stress-free academic planning! Easily organize your units, ensure you meet all prerequisites, and make smarter choices to stay ahead in your degree.

How Does It Work?

  • Easy Unit Selection - Quickly find and select your units with our instant search and prefilled plan options.
  • Intelligent Degree Planning - Automatically checks and alerts you to potential clashes and missing prerequisites.
  • Interactive Visualization - Visualize your degree path clearly and intuitively.
  • Social Insights - Check out what units your friends are taking, discover popular choices, and get inspired by others.

Key Features

  • Automatic Prerequisite Checks - No more surprises! Get alerts if prerequisites aren't met.
  • Popular Unit Recommendations - Discover the most popular electives and core units among peers.
  • Detailed Unit Info - Comprehensive information about each unit, including exam details, coordinators, and scheduling.
  • Collaborative Planning - Share your plans and easily view friends' schedules.
  • Seamless Experience - A clean, user-friendly interface designed for quick and easy use.
